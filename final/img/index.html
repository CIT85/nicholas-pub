Blueberry Pancakes

Preparation Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

About This Recipe

Blueberry pancakes are a classic sweet breakfast option that combine fluffy batter with bursts of fresh blueberry flavor.

See Recipe Instructions
Step-by-Step Instructions
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  2. Fold in fresh blueberries gently.
  3. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle until bubbles appear, then flip.
Tips for Best Results

Tip: Serve with maple syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
